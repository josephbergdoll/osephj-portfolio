Flow Clock

Identity, Print, Packaging
August 2012 — October 2012

The Flow clock was designed around the concept of Mental Flow, which is defined as:

"The mental state of operation in which a person performing an activity is fully immersed in a feeling of energized focus, full involvement, and enjoyment in the process of the activity."

It's based off of my own working process: the long gray hands (hours) represent the ideating process, while the short blue hands (minutes) then represent my creating process. This is symbolic of how, in mental flow, one is moving harmoniously in both thought and work. Finally, the Flow logotype serves as the seconds hand, which moves continuously rather than ticking and tocking.
