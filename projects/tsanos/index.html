Helen Tsanos Sheinman

Identity, Copywriting, Web
June 2012 — July 2012

Branding and identity system, press kit, web experience, product listing, and Wordpress journal for Cypriot author Helen Tsanos Sheinman. Her book, Love Laughter and Lunch is a collection of recipes that bring Cypriot flavor and tradition to the kitchen.

This project was featured on SiteInspire, where it was awarded a "Select" rating. It has also been featured on other online design outlets such as TheBestDesigns, OnePageLove, French Design Index, and Design Delight.

Done while at Piscatello Design Centre.
