Shift

Creative direction, web, print, motion
January 2013 — March 2013

Myself and four other senior design students assembled an exhibition and poster show, intended to communicate to younger students that with hard work and perseverance, anything is possible.

We opened SHIFT's fundraiser via IndieGoGo on February 5th, 2013, and exceeded our goal of $950 just three days later ($1600 was raised in total). The SHIFT exhibition and poster show opened on March 8th, 2013. A publication to share our stories and document our process in assembling the exhibiition is currently in production.

View the website at www.MakeAShift.co.
