Think Space

Research, Print, Web, Photography, Exhibit
August 2012 — May 2013
95 pages, 8"x10"

Think Space is the final product of my entire 2012-2013 senior thesis project. After researching why the modern office environment isn't conducive to productivity, I researched ways to make it better. The end result was a 95 page book, a case study, a website, and an exhibit shown at the ASU Visual Communication Design Senior Show in downtown Phoenix during May 2013.

View the site at www.Think-Space.co.
