AIGA ASU

Creative Direction, Identity, Web, Print
June 2012 — May 2013

While AIGA has maintained a student group on the Arizona State University campus for a few years now, its potential had never fully been realized. While I served as president during the 2012-2013 school year, AIGA ASU became a registered student organization with the university, committed to strengthening the connection between the students and local professional community. A logo and identity system were crafted, for both print and web applications, to help usher in this new beginning.
