GDSA x NYC

Print, Copywriting
February 2012 — March 2012
32 pages, 3.5"x5"

Materials for ASU's Graphic Design Student Association 2012 New York trip. The booklets contain the complete trip itinerary, background information on each of the firm visits, and subway routes to each studio. The t-shirts were silkscreened by hand and sold to fund trip scholarships for two students. After the trip, a thank-you poster was mailed to each of the studios we visited.

As seen on For Print Only on May 2, 2012.
